The Megalodon:

  • Literally means “big tooth” in Ancient Greek.
  • Is an extinct species of sharks.
  • Lived approximately 23 to 3.6 million years ago.
  • Is a close relative of the great white shark.
  • Is one of the largest and most powerful predators to have ever lived.
  • Maximum size and weight remain uncertain but could likely exceed 20 meters and 50 tons.
